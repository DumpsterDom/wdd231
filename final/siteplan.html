Smart Pantry

This is to help with an eeasy to follow name.

2. Site Purpose

Smart Pantry is a free web app with optional premium features that lets users track ingredients they have at home and instantly receive recipe suggestions based only on whats already in their pantry, fridge, and freezer. The site helps busy families, students, and eco-conscious users reduce food waste, save money, and decide whats for dinner in seconds.
